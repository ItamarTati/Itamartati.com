The Four Pillars of Getting a Software Engineer Job

This framework focuses on treating the job search as a structured process, ensuring you maximize both your volume and your quality across all stages.

1. Mass Apply: The Numbers Game

Your GoalApply to at least 5 jobs every day.
Why it WorksSoftware engineering job hunting is a numbers game.

2. Good Quality CV: The Metric-Driven Storyteller

Your FocusA CV that talks about your accomplishments through metrics.

3. Good Portfolio: Production-Ready Proof

Your RequirementPortfolio needs to have professional production-ready projects.

4. Good Interview Skills: Confident Execution

Your DefinitionA good interviewer is someone who speaks confidently, is prepared, and can code.
